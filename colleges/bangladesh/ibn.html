MBBS From Ibn Sina Medical College

Ibn Sina Medical College

East-West Medical College & Hospital is a leading medical institute in Bangladesh. The institute is located at Aichi Nagar, JBCS Saroni, Khairtak, Turag, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is situated at the bank of river Turag with natural flourishing surroundings and five kilometers away from Hazrat Shah Jalal International airport. The college is to provide on excellent broad based education with a focus on professional development for the students to equip them with knowledge, skills and attitude necessary not only to address the priority health problems of the community but also to acquire a firm basis for future training and studies.
The aim of the East-West Medical College is to teach and train the selected Students for five academic years to offer MBBS degree conducted by and dedicated teachers to create graduate doctors capable of teaching distance population within natural arena can serve the population as per need? highly qualified to newly designed community Oriented medical education including MBBS course. To arrange a training course for the young doctors & other staffs. To provide postgraduate training and research with a view to qualified postgraduate doctors. To meet the responsibilities of providing preventive as well as curative health care to the people of Bangladesh.
The academic and Hospital building of the institute is built on its own land of Alim Foundation, having modern classrooms, lecture galleries, practical classrooms, departmental museums, library, reading rooms and teachers corner etc. It also accommodates a cafeteria, a Mosque and separate common rooms for male and female students with facilities for indoor and outdoor games. There are separate hostels for boys and girls consisting a total 1050 seats of which 550 seats are for female and 500 seats are for male students.
