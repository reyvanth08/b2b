MBBS From Asian Medical University

Asian Medical Institute

Established in 2004, Asian Medical Institute ranked among the top 10 medical universities in Kyrgyzstan and ranks 30th among all universities in Kyrgyzstan. The university has so much to offer for Indian students.
There are more than 2000 Indian students already pursuing medical studies from Asian Medical Institute and more than 15000 students have pursued their medical degrees from this university.
Asian Medical Institute is an NMC (National Medical Commission) approved university which makes it trustworthy for Indian students. There are 3 operating campuses of Asian Medical University.
Asian Medical Institute offers Indian students special coaching for the preparation NMC screening test from the first year. Indian professors are allotted for the students to help them and teach them for the same. They also help students to update Indian medical developments and news. The teaching language is English which makes learning much easier.

Why Study MBBS at Asian Medical Institute?

  • Asian Medical Institute has an affordable fee structure for Indian students i.e 2.24 lakh per year.
  • It is the top-ranked University with quality education.
  • The medium of teaching at the Asian Medical Institute is English which makes Indian students understand better.
  • Asian medical institute is worldwide recognized and also NMC approved, so students can practice in India after completing a medical degree in the Asian Medical Institute.
  • Asian Medical Institute has minimum eligibility criteria for Indian students. Students need not qualify IELTS or TOEFL.
  • The institute has 8 hospitals reserved for the students to teach perfectly.

    • Eligibility Criteria

    Check out the eligibility below:

    • You are at least 17 and maximum 25 years old on or before 31st December of the admission year.
    • You have passed class 12th with PCB and English subjects from a board recognized by the authorities in India.
    • You have secured a minimum of 50% in PCB in 10+2.
    • You have a qualified NEET Exam.
