MBBS From Mkhitar Gosh Armenian-Russian International University

Mkhitar Gosh Armenian-Russian International University

Mkhitar Gosh Armenian-Russian International University was established in 1996 in Yerevan. The university has accreditation of Armenia’s National Centre for Professional Education Quality Assurance known as ANQA and it is also officially approved by the National Medical Commission which complies with the standards set by NMC.
The university is one of the top universities in Armenia and it is known for providing high quality education. For the International Recognition of Equivalence of State Diplomas and Degrees, the university is a recognized member of UNESCO.
The MBBS program offered by the University is approved by the Ministry of Education and Science of Armenia Indian students who complete their graduation from the university are eligible to sit for NMC screening test and practice in India as a doctor.

Benifits of MBBS at Mkhitar Gosh Armenian-Russian International University:

  • A student can apply for internships in various hospitals in Armenia, after completing his/her first year of studies.
  • The graduate degree which students get in MBBS from Mkhitar Gosh University is valid in India and other countries.
  • Students can practice medicine in any part of the world once they qualify for screening tests of the National Medical Commission (NMC).
  • High-quality education in MBBS courses is ensured through the combination of modern medicine and practical training.
  • The university has approval from the National Medical Commission (NMC).
  • The Mkhitar Gosh university follows a curriculum similar to that of MBBS in India. Thus, Indian students find it easy to mix up in the new environment and the education system of Mkhitar Gosh University.
  • Language is not a barrier because English is the medium of instruction while studying MBBS in the Mkhitar Gosh university.
  • No need to clear IELTS or TOEFL for admission into Mkhitar Gosh University.

    • Eligibility Criteria

    Students must meet the following requirements set by the National Medical Commission (NMC) for admission into the Mkhitar Gosh Armenian-Russian International University:

  • You are at least 17 and maximum 25 years old on or before 31st December of the admission year.
  • You have passed class 12th with PCB and English subjects from a board recognized by the authorities in India.
  • You have secured a minimum of 50% in PCB in 10+2.
  • You have a qualified NEET Exam.

    • Documents Required at the Time of Admission

    Following documents are required for admission to Mkhitar Gosh Armenian-Russian International University:

    • Scanned copy of the filled Application Form
    • Scanned copy of passing certificate and mark sheet of 12th
    • Scanned copy of passing certificate of class 10th
    • 8 colour photograph with white background 3”4”
    • Scanned copy of passport (first and last page)
    • Original Passport
    • Original 12th mark sheet and certificate
    • Enrolment/Admission Letter
    • Duly filled admission form
    • Scanned copy of NEET Result
