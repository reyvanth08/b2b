MBBS From Bogomolets National Medical University

Bogomolets National Medical University

In 1841, Bogomolets National Medical University was established in the city of Kyiv, Ukraine. It is the one of foremost university to study MBBS in Ukraine.
The medical faculty of university reorganized in 1920, The first lecture of Bogomolets National Medical University was started on 9th September, 1941. After the physiologist Alexander A Bogomolets, the Bogomolets was named as O.O. Bogomolets National Medical University.
The course of study in Medical Faculty is 5 years and in some other faculty is 4 year. The university itself is the broadband destination for students from all over the world.
The Bogomolets university is one of the best medical university among the top ranked universities in the world. The Institutes not only provides a center of excellence in Medical field also it is the best for the center of learning. The University also provides the art of research facilities and equipment to students.

Why Studying MBBS in Bogomolets Medical University?

  • Bogomolets National Medical University provides low-cost medical education to students.
  • The institute is a part of Bologna Process (Bologna Process is a series of agreement with European Universities to establish a high-quality standard in the field of Medical Education).
  • The degree offered by the Bogomolets Medical University is authorized by International bodies and Organizations like NMC, WHO & many more.
  • Globally acclaimed staff and working professionals are always there to share their knowledge and experience with the students.
  • Guest lectures, seminars, and the research activities are organized prominently.
  • About 10,000 students studying medicine in Ukraine. Bogomolets University is the foremost platform for those students.
  • Bilateral student agreements with the reputed countries such as Sweden, Finland making ease of study in Ukraine for Students.
  • Emphasis on ongoing training programs and practical methodology.

    • Eligibility Criteria

    Check out the eligibility below:

  • Minimum of 75% marks in English and also equivalent in exams such as TOEFL or IELTS is mandatory.
  • Minimum 75% of aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
  • Students should have to mail a scanned copy of documents to the Institute.
  • Age should be 17 ye `ars before 31st December of the year of the admission process.

    • Documents Required

    For the admission process the Medical aspirants must carry all the following related documents given below:

    1. Scanned copy of passport
    2. Scanned copy of Birth Certificate
    3. Scanned copy of 10th Marksheet
    4. Scanned copy of 12th Marksheet
    5. School Leaving Certificate
    6. Police Verification Certificate
    7. Medical Certificate
    8. NEET Score Card
