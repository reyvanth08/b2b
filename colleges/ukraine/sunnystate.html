MBBS From Sumy State University

Sumy State University

In the year 1948, Sumy State University was established in Sumy Oblast, Ukraine. Today, it is the leading university of a classical type with the III-IV accreditation level in the region.
Presently, the University has about 15,000 students from almost 50 countries across the world, who are pursuing pre- bachelor, bachelors and masters degrees in 22 fields of knowledge & 51 majors.
Sumy State University is one of the best universities for studying MBBS in Ukraine. Sumy State University offers 12 courses across 5 streams namely Medical, Engineering, Law, Media & Mass Communication, Management, and across 5 degrees like MBBS, B.Tech, LLB, BBA, BA.

Why Studying MBBS in Sumy State University?

  • It is approved by NMC and WHO.
  • Studying MBBS at Sumy State University requires a low cost than MBBS in India.
  • The duration of the MBBS program at Sumy State University is 6 years and the course is taught in full English medium of instruction.
  • Students can learn multiple cultures and languages as 1300 foreign students from 50 different countries are studying at the university.
  • Sumy State University is one of the oldest colleges for MBBS in Ukraine.
  • The university has exchange programs and partnerships with numerous reputed medical universities across the world.
  • Indian students studying MBBS at Sumy State University get ample clinical exposure from the 3rd year of their classes and can interact with patients at the very early stage of their education.
  • Hostels and Indian mess are available for international students.

    • Eligibility Criteria

    To study MBBS at Sumy State Medical University, below given is the eligibility criteria:

  • The candidate must be 17 years old on or before 31st December of the year in which the admission is being taken.
  • The Nationality of the students must be ‘Indian’.
  • The applicants must have cleared the NEET exam.
  • All the candidates must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as elective subjects and acquired at least half in a total of PCB joined. Also, students must have studied and passed English as an obligatory subject.
  • The candidate must have scored a minimum of 50% of marks in subjects like Physics, Biology, Chemistry combined.
