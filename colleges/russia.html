Studying MBBS in Russia is substantial for students who want to pursue Medicine in Europe. From the 16th century, the history of Medical Science in Russia is rated at the top compared to other countries. The quality education by the top MBBS colleges in Russia is the only reason behind it.
The Russian government has subsidized Medical education and tuition fees for Indian students by 50% of actual education. This helped to encourage foreign students to take admission to Russia for an MBBS degree.
30 out of the top 100 medical universities worldwide belong to Russia, this states that promising and quality education is provided by each medical institute in Russia.
The medical universities of Russia offer affordable fees and well-furnished accommodations for students. Well equipped laboratories, promising education, quality guidance to students, the value of a Russian MBBS degree in India, these factors, and success stories of alumni lead to an increase in admissions for MBBS courses in Russia.
The degree awarded to students after completion of the Medicine course in Russia is M.D (Physician), which is equivalent to MBBS in India.
