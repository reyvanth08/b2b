What Defines Shoolin?

Shoolin shows you the way.

As one of the leading education consultants in Kerala, Anix Education was founded in 2006 with the goal of making global education available to students all around the world. By helping students secure world class education within reasonable budget, Anix has emerged as the most respected foreign education consultants in the country. Having aided thousands of students pursue their dream careers in ideal countries, Anix Education takes immense pride in providing consultation to candidates from all walks of life. Following its modest origins at the heart of Kochi, Anix Education has branched out to offer a broad range of consultation services to students everywhere. With consultation centers in major centers of the world and associations in over 20 countries, Anix education boasts a long tradition of affiliating with as many as 300 universities. Operating across India in major hub locations, Anix Education is equipped with a host of affiliated centers all over the United Arab Emirates (UAE). Aided by a powerful network of experienced consultants and educators, Anix Education continues to make life-changing influence in the lives of students.

Free Counselling

Shoolin has a team of trained and expert counselors, who provide help to identify the professional goals, enabling the student to take a wiser academic decision. These counselors give Personal Guidance to choose courses that perfectly fits your Career or Personal Goals. We also host direct interviews with the Russian University representatives and provide you with all-inclusive information on various study topics.

University Selection

Shoolin counselors help you to select the right University across the world according to the need and requirement of the student. We look at our students’ academic documents to make sure it is as per the norms specified by the University. Each student is given a comprehensive overview of program details along with it we help them choose the right program.

Documentation Assistance

  • Passport Assistance: Shoolin assists you completely while applying for the passport. We provide you with the admission letter that helps in acquiring the passport on Tatkal(immediate) Basis.
  • Education Loan: Shoolin provides you with all the necessary documentations which provide assistance in getting the educational loans.

Pre-Departure Guidance

Every year, Shoolin conducts SOBRANIYE, a get together of students and parents before the departure. In this session, they get to know about the ins and outs of the respective country and traveling guidelines. Also, we let you contact the other students who are already studying in the respective countries.

Travel Assistance

At Shoolin, we have a separate department called RUS TOURISM which provides you with the best possible options to travel. We help you identify the right airline for excess baggage schemes. The department helps you in your entire course period in traveling. We also arrange travel for parents and relatives of the student when required.

Post Departure Services

  • We accompany our student from the Airport to the University.
  • We provide the accommodation facilities to our students till they get a hostel room.
  • We assist you in your hostel registration and allotting you the room.
