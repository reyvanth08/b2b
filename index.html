What interprets Shoolin Education Group?

Shoolin Education Group shows you the path

Shoolin Education Group is an education advisor who helps medical aspirants to follow their goal of becoming a doctor.

The growing demand for medical seats in India has created difficulty to fulfil a large number of medical aspirants and as I have seen most of them give up their ambitions. So students need a legal and ethical organisation that can help them to pursue their dreams.

Shoolin Education Group is an organisation certified by the government of India and more importantly, it is run by the medical student himself who is very much familiar with the area.

Every year from Karnataka around 1.2lakhs students apply for NEET exam and around 60,000 gets qualified but there are nearly only 9000 medical seats for the state in total so what do the rest of the other 50,000 medical aspirants do?

This is the main reason why the Shoolin Education Group is founded. We give more preference for the medical aspirants from Karnataka. The main branch of the organisation is based in Bangalore, India.

Services We Offer

Free Counselling

Applying for higher education is a huge decision both financially and emotionally so we understand the requirements of the students and give personal guidance to perfectly match their ability, concerns, educational and financial background.

University Selection

Shoolin Education Group helps you to choose the right university across the world according to the need and necessities of the students. We look at our student’s educational documents to make sure it is as per the standards specified by the university.

Documentation Assistance

We assist you totally while applying for the passport and provide you with all essential documentation that will assist you in getting an educational loan if necessary; we help you with the attestation, translation and all other documentation procedure.

Pre-Departure Guidance

We are planning to host a get together of students and parents before the departure. In this session, we give complete guidance about the country and travelling information.

Travel Assistance

We give students the best possible options to travel by helping you identify the right airlines for extra baggage and be in contact with the student the entire journey.

Post Departure Services

Shoolin Education Group ensures that you have a hassle-free transition in the country and we provide you services like airport pick up, accommodation, SIM card and internet access. We help students during the entire procedure from admission till getting graduated.
