About Us

Shoolin Education Group, a premier organization in South India was founded in 2021, with a vision to provide quality, economically viable education abroad. Anix Education takes great pride in being the pioneer organization to provide admission to worldwide. The counselling and guidance from expert counsellors and eminent representatives from Universities help the students make the right choice. Our exclusive services ranges from Universities / Colleges shortlisting to application submission, liaising with Universities, visa assistance scholarship guidance, financial aid, education loans, to pre departure and post landing assistance. We keep in touch with our students during the course till the graduation. Anix Education takes an active part in their cultural, sporting and academic activities apart from everyday studies. We organize get together, parties, competitions, festivals and celebrations. Anix Education stands out with good customer base ensuring support and remedy for all grievances throughout the course. The good will and trust earned from the students and the parents has paved way to expand the admission to various leading universities.
